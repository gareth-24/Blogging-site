Mariners gameday blog

Spring training 3/14 vs Kansas City Royals

Starting Pitcher: Logan Gilbert

Logan Gilbert took to the mound in his final outing of this spring. He stuggled with command out of the gate allowing a run on a walk, stolen base, and single in the top of the first. However, he found his footing and shut out the Royals over the next two frames.

Offensive summary:

  • Kolton Wong 2 RBI single off of Brad Keller

  • Jarred Kelenic RBI double off of Mike Mayers

  • Tom Murphy RBI doubleoff off of Mike Mayers

  • Mike Ford solo homerun off of Brooks Kriske

  • Randy Bednar 3-run homerun off of Leonardo Rivas

Final Score: KC 4 - SEA 8
